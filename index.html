Kazi Nazurl Islam

The national poet of Bangladesh

Rebel Poet

Kazi Nazurl Islam is the national poet of Bangladesh who is
especially recognised as a Bidrohi Kobi(rebel poet) arroutd the
country. Nazurl was a famous poet, musucian and philosopher of
Bangladesh. The famous poet Nazurl's writings deal with the
revolution of that period of the British government. His
revolutionary attempt helped him to be the national poet of
Bangladesh. He has raised his voice against any kind of fascism and
oppression and got popularity swiftly arround the Indian
Subcontinent.

Awsome Writer

Nazurl's writings explored themes such as love, freedom, humanity,
and revolution. He opposed all forms of bigotry and
fundamentalism, including religious, cast-based and gender-based.
Throught his career, Nazurl wrote short stories, novels and
essays but is best known for his songs and poems. He pioneered new
music forms such as Bengali ghazals. Nazurl wrote and composed
music for neary 4,000 songs(many recorded on HMV and
gramophone records), collectively known as Nazurl Geeti.

"Love as no meaning and amount."-Kazi Nazrul Islam
